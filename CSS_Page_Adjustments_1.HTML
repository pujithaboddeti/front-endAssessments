Add padding and margin with different size and understand the difference between them. HTML: Padding and Margin

Padding vs. Margin

Padding Box
Margin Box
Mixed Padding/Margin
Individual Padding/Margin

Padding: Creates space inside an element, between the element's content and its border. It affects the element's background.

Margin: Creates space outside an element, between the element's border and other elements. It does not affect the element's background.
